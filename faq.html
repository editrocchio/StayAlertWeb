Crime Descriptions

Break and Enter (Commercial)

Breaking and entering into a commercial property with intent to commit an offence.

Break and Enter (Residential)

Breaking and entering into a dwelling/house/apartment/garage with intent to commit an offence.

Other Theft

Theft of property that includes personal items (purse, wallet, cellphone, laptop, etc.), bicycle, etc.

Mischief

A person commits mischief that willfully causes malicious destruction, damage, or defacement of property. This also includes any public mischief towards another person.

Theft of Vehicle

Theft of a vehicle, motorcycle, or any motor vehicle.

Theft From Vehicle

Theft of property from a vehicle.

Theft of Bycicle

Theft of a bicycle.

Vehicle Collision or Pedestrian Struck (injury)

Includes all categories of vehicle involved accidents with injuries. This includes pedestrian and cyclist involved incidents with injuries.

Vehicle Collision or Pedestrian Struck (fatality)

Includes primarily pedestrian or cyclist struck and killed by a vehicle. It also includes vehicle to vehicle fatal accidents.
